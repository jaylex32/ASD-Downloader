Privacy Policy for ATHENA HLS DETECTOR

Last updated: [Current Date]

ATHENA HLS DETECTOR ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

Our extension collects and stores the following information:

  • Video URLs detected on webpages you visit
  • Playlist information you create within the extension

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the purpose of providing the core functionality of our extension, which includes:

  • Detecting and displaying video URLs
  • Managing and playing your created playlists

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not transmit any of your data to external servers.

4. Your Rights

You can delete all data stored by the extension at any time by removing the extension from your browser.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
